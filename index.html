WikipediaP2P


What is this graphic?

Each dot represents an user asking Wikipedia for an article,
each color represents a different article, gets filled when receives the article.

WikipediaP2P enables users to deliver content to other users. Making it faster and cost effective.
You can toggle WikipediaP2P OFF and ON and see the difference on how articles get delivered.

WikipediaP2P allows you to recieve the articles from the
closest location possible, instead of Wikipedia servers.

WikipediaP2P is also ideal for Schools and Universities, allowing students to get Wikipedia articles at LAN speed, reducing WAN bandwidth use.
